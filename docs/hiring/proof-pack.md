# Proof Pack — Competitive Differentiation

## Goal
Provide hard evidence that this agent can execute the RevenueCat role as a compounding system, not a one-off content generator.

## Why this stands out
Most applications will be strong in one area (content OR growth OR tooling). This system is built to combine all three in one weekly loop:

1. Technical content production
2. Growth experimentation
3. Community signal capture
4. Product feedback synthesis
5. Weekly KPI reporting and strategy iteration

## Evidence Index

### A) Technical Content Capability (2 examples/week target)
- Planned artifact 1: RevenueCat integration tutorial (agent-first workflow)
- Planned artifact 2: Growth case-study with instrumentation

Acceptance criteria:
- Includes code or implementation detail
- Includes measurable objective and audience
- Includes distribution plan

### B) Growth Experiment Capability (1 experiment/week target)
- Planned artifact: full experiment spec + readout

Required fields:
- Hypothesis
- Primary KPI + guardrail KPI
- Distribution channel
- Timebox
- Result interpretation
- Next-step decision

### C) Community Engagement Capability (50+ interactions/week target)
- Planned artifact: meaningful interaction ledger

Meaningful interaction rubric:
- Adds technical value OR decision value
- Includes follow-up opportunity
- Includes classification (question, unblock, insight, objection)

### D) Product Feedback Capability (3+ items/week target)
- Planned artifact: structured product feedback packets

Packet structure:
- Context
- Reproduction / scenario
- Impact framing
- Suggested solution direction
- Priority rationale

### E) Reporting Capability (weekly async check-in)
- Planned artifact: weekly report template with KPI scorecard and learnings

Report includes:
- What shipped
- KPI results vs target
- Misses and root causes
- Next-week plan

## Competitive Positioning Statement

This agent stands out by proving **operational repeatability** and **cross-functional compounding output**:
- Not just writing content, but testing distribution.
- Not just engaging community, but translating it into product insight.
- Not just shipping tasks, but reporting outcomes and improving each week.

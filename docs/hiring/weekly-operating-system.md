# Weekly Operating System

## Required Weekly Outputs

- 2 content pieces minimum (technical, tutorial, docs, or growth case-study)
- 1 new growth experiment minimum
- 50+ meaningful community interactions
- 3+ structured product feedback items
- 1 async weekly report with metrics and learnings

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Core Focus | Skill Owner | Required Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Docs/API refresh + content backlog | rc-docs-api-ingestion | Updated ingestion notes and prioritized topics |
| Tuesday | Content production cycle A | rc-technical-content-engine | Content piece #1 draft/final |
| Wednesday | Growth experiment launch | rc-growth-experiment-lab | Experiment brief and launch record |
| Thursday | Community engagement + feedback synthesis | rc-community-engagement-ops / rc-product-feedback-analyst | Interaction ledger update + 3 feedback packets |
| Friday | Content production cycle B + weekly reporting | rc-technical-content-engine / rc-weekly-metrics-reporter | Content piece #2 + async report |

## KPI Thresholds

- Content pieces: `>=2`
- Experiments: `>=1`
- Meaningful interactions: `>=50`
- Product feedback items: `>=3`
- Weekly report: `1` published before end of week

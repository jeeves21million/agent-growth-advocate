# Payment and Contract Model (Operator + Agent)

## Purpose
Define a practical contractor model for an autonomous agent role while preserving normal business/legal expectations.

## Legal/Payment Structure
- The human operator (or operator LLC) is the legal contractor and invoice recipient.
- The agent is the execution system used by the contractor to deliver services.

## Compensation Structure (example)
- Contract term: 6 months
- Total compensation: per offer terms
- Invoice cadence: monthly (or biweekly)
- Payment terms: Net 15 / Net 30

## Compute/API Budget Responsibility (explicit)
Per the job posting, RevenueCat states the offer includes:
- **"A dedicated budget for compute resources and API access."**

Accordingly, compute/token/API costs required for role execution are expected to be funded by RevenueCat under agreed budget guardrails.

Recommended operating terms:
- RevenueCat is budget owner for model/API spend used for contracted work.
- Approved provider/model list and monthly budget cap are defined up front.
- Overage requires explicit approval before additional spend.
- Weekly usage reporting includes token/compute cost by workflow.

## Acceptance and Proof of Delivery
Each invoice references an evidence bundle:
- content artifacts
- experiment logs/readouts
- feedback packets
- weekly async reports
- supporting commit/run logs

## IP and Ownership (critical)
All work product created for the engagement is treated as **work made for hire** (where applicable) and/or fully assigned to RevenueCat under the contractor agreement.

This includes, without limitation:
- written content, tutorials, docs, and code samples
- growth experiment designs and outputs
- media assets (images, videos, GIFs, scripts)
- product feedback documents and reporting artifacts
- automation scripts, templates, and workflow materials produced for RevenueCat

Operator and agent retain only:
- pre-existing tooling/framework know-how
- general skills and non-confidential techniques

No reuse of RevenueCat confidential material outside engagement scope.

## Non-Payment Safeguards
- Late payment clause and notice window
- Right to pause non-critical work for overdue invoices
- Audit-ready artifact ledger for delivery disputes

## Confidentiality and Security
- NDA-compliant handling of confidential data
- Least-privilege credentials
- Logged access and action trails
- Revocation-ready integration model at contract end

# RC Product Feedback Analyst

## Trigger Criteria

Use when identifying friction points, feature opportunities, or roadmap signals from hands-on usage and community conversations, especially to satisfy the 3+ weekly feedback target.

## Workflow

1. Confirm task scope, success metric, and deadline.
2. Execute the role-specific process in `references/workflow.md`.
3. Produce concrete artifacts and validation notes.
4. Handoff using `references/handoff-contract.md` to rc-weekly-metrics-reporter.

## Posting Alignment

This skill directly supports RevenueCat's Agentic AI Developer & Growth Advocate responsibilities.
